MENU 01 - TOY SOLDIERS

long before we opened our doors for the first time as trap. cocktail bar, the space we occupy was claimed as a gallery by one of adelaide’s first successful art groups specialising in graffiti and street art known as the toy soldiers. disbanding in the mid-2010's, their commission work can still be found at miscellaneous places around adelaide. for our first official menu, we inspire five characters commonly found in their work and bring them back to life in the space that was once their home.

reviews.

Adrian, ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Heard music filtering up from underground (literally) and followed the sound into Cold Chisel Alley to find the coolest /artiest/funkiest little cocktail bar I’ve been in for years. By happy coincidence it was ‘Hip Hop Open Mic Night’ (last Wed of each month). Cosy chic place with a great crowd, fabulous band and intimate energy. Cocktails were delicious. Spent a couple of hours enjoying guys and gals passing the mic around and busting rhymes. Felt like everyone knew each other and we were among friends. I’ve already put it my calendar for next month. Highly recommended!

M T, ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

For those seeking a one-of-a-kind drinking experience, this hidden gem delivers in spades, offering a haven for cocktail enthusiasts looking to escape the ordinary and indulge in something extraordinary.

4chillies, ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

This is a great concept. A bar that deliberately has a small amount of seats so that everyone gets great attention! The cocktails were delicious and the atmosphere was welcoming. I love that you can draw on a coaster and then those become the drink coasters - a very personal touch.
