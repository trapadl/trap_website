SHIT THAT JUST TASTES GOOD
==========================

1. LIMONCELLO GRANITA
---------------------
Price: $24
Hero Spirit: Storytellers Cocktail Helper (Lemon Peel Infused)

Ingredients:
- 30ml Lemon Peel Cocktail Helper
- 20ml Lemon Oleo
- 10ml Lemon Juice
- 140ml Lemon Myrtle Soda
- 35 drops Citra Hop Bitters

Method: Batch and carbonate. Serve over cubed/crushed ice. Garnish with lemon myrtle dust and apply 35 drops of citra hop bitters on top before serving.

Homemade Ingredient Components:
- Lemon Peel Cocktail Helper: lemon peel, cocktail helper spirit
- Lemon Oleo: lemon peel, sugar, sugar syrup
  Note: Made by macerating lemon peel with sugar to extract oils (oleo saccharum), then combining with sugar syrup.
- Lemon Myrtle Soda: lemon myrtle flakes, water
  Note: Made by steeping lemon myrtle flakes in room temperature water for 3 hours, then straining.
- Citra Hop Bitters: cocktail helper spirit, citrus hops
  Note: Made by infusing citrus hops in cocktail helper for one hour, then diluting and straining twice.

Note on Citra Hop Bitters: The bitters add a bright, aromatic citrus-forward bitterness that lifts the drink and balances the sweetness of the oleo and soda.

4. MANGO PANNA COTTA
--------------------
Price: $24
Hero Spirit: Storytellers Distillery Vol 1 Australian Dry Gin

Ingredients:
- 10ml Storytellers Distillery Vol 1 Gin
- 10ml Dolin Blanco Vermouth
- 10ml Cocktail Helper
- 10ml Sugar Syrup
- 120ml Panna Cotta Water
- Mango Float (float)

Method: Pour batch (180ml) into highball 90% filled with ice. Top with Mango Float.

Homemade Ingredient Components:
- Panna Cotta Water: coconut water, milk powder, frozen mango, sugar, citric acid
- Mango Float: frozen mango, coconut water, citric acid, xanthan gum
  Note: The xanthan gum thickens the mango mixture so it floats on top of the drink rather than mixing in, creating the layered panna cotta visual effect.


5. BANANA CREAM ESPRESSO MARTINI
--------------------------------
Price: $23
Hero Spirit: Storytellers Cocktail Helper

Ingredients:
- 30ml Cocktail Helper
- 30ml Sugar Syrup
- 60ml Cold Brew Coffee
- 40g Hot Banana Cream (garnish)

Method: Batch and refrigerate to settle. Build cold ingredients from freezer into coupe. Top with hot banana cream (siphoned).

Note: This is essentially the same recipe as a regular espresso martini, but instead of shaking it, the base is simply poured cold and topped with the hot banana cream for temperature and textural contrast.

Homemade Ingredient Components:
- Hot Banana Cream: coconut milk, coconut cream, heavy cream, sugar syrup, banana liqueur
  Note: The cream mixture is charged in a siphon (like a whipped cream dispenser) with N2O cartridges to aerate it into a light, foamy texture. The siphon is kept in a warm water bath during service to maintain temperature. Must be checked every morning for quality as it's quite temperamental once it's been heated and cooled.
