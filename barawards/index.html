Best Cocktail Bar South Australia 2024 🏆
🏆 Help Us Go Back-to-Back!

Last year we took home Best Cocktail Bar South Australia 2024 — and we're going for it again!

What a year it's been at trap. We've launched two new menus with our beloved Toy Soldiers concept seeing our most loved 'robot' cocktail travel an incredible 22km over our bar top. Our innovative Guardians of the Cocktail menu worked tirelessly to combat cost of living pressures, lowering our average cocktail price from $24 to $19 while maintaining exceptional trap. quality and hospitality.

Every single day we focus on providing the best drinks, service and chat we can. Having recognition amongst Australia's best food and beverage venues means the world to us and helps bring new faces through our doors. If you've enjoyed what we do, please consider voting for us again in this year's Bartender Magazine Bar Awards!

🗳️ How to Vote

Voting Instructions:

  1. Use the ← → arrows (or swipe on mobile) to navigate these info cards.
  2. Move down the form below and for each category, click the "Copy" button from the info card if you want a suggestion.
  3. Paste the copied text into the corresponding field in the official form below.
  4. Fill in all required fields in the form to submit your vote.

Note: The official survey requires votes in all categories. We've provided suggestions for each to make it quick and easy for you!
